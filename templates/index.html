Folding prefabricated building

A folding prefabricated house is a type of prefabricated house that is designed with a collapsible structure. This design allows the house to be quickly unfolded and stowed away when needed, making it easy to move and store.

Double Wing Expansion Room

Double-wing expansion box is a new type of box house product made by folding technology. Its main feature is that it has a double-wing structure that can be expanded, and this design makes the box room remain compact and portable, and at the same time, it can be flexibly expanded according to the actual demand, so as to provide a more spacious space to meet different residential and commercial needs.

Packing house

The Packing Box is a modular building based on a structural system of steel frames and lightweight wall panels. Packing Box consists of several unit modules that can be quickly installed, moved and transported in a very convenient way, easy to dismantle and store. The important point is that it is environmentally friendly. This is why packing boxes are more common nowadays and mobile homes are rare.

Power storage box

Power storage boxes are widely used in power systems, industrial and mining enterprises, logistics giants and other fields, and play an important role in grid peak and valley balance, emergency backup, new energy peak shifting and other aspects. With the rapid development and application of renewable energy, the power storage box will be more popularized and improved, and become one of the important means to promote the development of clean energy and improve the efficiency of energy utilization.

Packing box office building

Packing box office building is a common temporary building, with packing box housing as the unit base, with easy installation, short construction period, can be packaged for long-distance transportation and other characteristics, commonly used in construction sites, medical rescue, field barracks, resettlement housing and a variety of commercial housing.

Folding Splicing Room

Folding splicing room is a kind of flexible and adaptable building form, which is spliced by a single folding room and can be freely combined according to the size of the space used.
